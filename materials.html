Materials

Every material we offer is weatherproof, UV-resistant, and built to last. Choose the one that matches your vision.

Most Popular

White Vinyl

The industry standard for a reason. Our premium white vinyl delivers crisp, vibrant colors on a clean white background. Perfect for logos, illustrations, and any design where color accuracy matters.

  • Thickness: 3.4 mil
  • Finish: Gloss, Matte, or Satin
  • Durability: 5+ years outdoor
  • Best for: Logos, packaging, laptops, water bottles

Clear Vinyl

Invisible until you notice it. Clear vinyl lets your design float on any surface, blending seamlessly with the background. Ideal for windows, glass, and when you want a premium, understated look.

  • Thickness: 3.0 mil
  • Finish: Gloss or Matte
  • Durability: 4+ years outdoor
  • Best for: Windows, glass, product labels, minimalist designs

Eye-Catching

Holographic

The rainbow effect that stops people mid-scroll. Our holographic material shifts through the entire color spectrum as light hits it from different angles. Makes any design feel premium and collectible.

  • Thickness: 3.5 mil
  • Pattern: Linear rainbow shift
  • Durability: 3+ years outdoor
  • Best for: Limited editions, merch, brand stickers, collectibles

Glitter

Tiny flecks of sparkle embedded throughout. Unlike surface glitter that flakes off, our glitter material has the sparkle sealed inside the vinyl. All the shine, none of the mess.

  • Thickness: 4.0 mil
  • Flake size: Fine micro-glitter
  • Durability: 3+ years outdoor
  • Best for: Party supplies, cosmetics, fashion, kids products

Mirror Chrome

Actual reflective chrome finish. This material creates a true mirror effect that reflects light and surroundings. High-impact and unmistakably premium. Your design becomes a statement piece.

  • Thickness: 3.5 mil
  • Finish: High-polish mirror
  • Durability: 3+ years outdoor
  • Best for: Automotive, luxury brands, tech products, special editions

Kraft Paper

The natural, unbleached look of brown paper. Kraft gives your stickers an organic, handmade aesthetic that feels honest and approachable. Perfect for brands with an eco-conscious or artisanal identity.

  • Thickness: 3.0 mil
  • Texture: Natural paper grain
  • Durability: 2+ years indoor, 1 year outdoor
  • Best for: Food packaging, organic brands, handmade goods, farmers markets

Specialty

Glow in the Dark

Charges in light, glows in darkness. Our glow material absorbs ambient light and releases it as a bright green glow when the lights go out. The effect lasts for hours and recharges infinitely.

  • Thickness: 4.5 mil
  • Glow duration: 8+ hours
  • Durability: 3+ years outdoor
  • Best for: Safety, events, kids products, novelty items

Brushed Aluminum

Industrial texture meets premium finish. The brushed aluminum look adds sophistication and a tactile quality to your stickers. Creates a subtle shimmer that catches light as it moves.

  • Thickness: 3.5 mil
  • Texture: Linear brushed pattern
  • Durability: 4+ years outdoor
  • Best for: Tech products, industrial brands, equipment labels, automotive
