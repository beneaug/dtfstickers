Everything you need to know about ordering stickers from 12oz.

Die-cut stickers are cut precisely to the shape of your design, with no extra backing material around the edges. They're perfect for logos, characters, and custom shapes where you want the sticker to match your artwork exactly.

Kiss-cut stickers are cut through the vinyl layer but leave the backing paper intact in a square or rectangle shape. This makes them easier to peel and apply, and they're great for handouts, packaging inserts, and retail display.
We accept PNG, JPG, PDF, AI (Adobe Illustrator), EPS, and SVG files. For best results, we recommend:
  • Vector files (AI, EPS, SVG, PDF) for the sharpest print quality
  • Raster images at 300 DPI or higher at your final print size
  • CMYK color mode for accurate color reproduction
  • Including a 1/16" bleed if your design goes to the edge

If your file doesn't meet these requirements, don't worry! We'll reach out to work with you on getting the best possible result.
