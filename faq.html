Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering custom stickers.

Ordering

No minimums. Order 1 sticker or 100,000. However, pricing improves significantly at higher quantities - you'll see the best per-unit prices at 250+ stickers.

Die-cut stickers are cut precisely to the shape of your design. No backing visible - just your artwork. Best for logos and custom shapes.

Kiss-cut stickers keep a rectangular backing around your design. Easier to peel and great for handouts, packaging inserts, or selling.

Yes. Every order gets a free digital proof via email showing exactly how your sticker will look. We won't print until you approve it. Need a physical sample? We can arrange that for a small fee.

Artwork

We accept PNG, JPG, PDF, AI, EPS, and SVG files.

For best results: vector files (AI, EPS, SVG) or raster images at 300 DPI minimum. If your file isn't perfect, we'll work with you to get the best result.

We're printers, not designers - but we can help with minor adjustments like resizing, color correction, or adding bleed. For major design work, we recommend working with a designer first.

Our white vinyl has a white base, so white areas in your design will print as white. For clear or holographic materials, we can print a white underbase to make colors pop. Just note this in your order.

Materials

Yes. All our vinyl materials are waterproof, UV-resistant, and dishwasher safe. They're built for outdoor use and rated to last 3-5 years depending on the material.

Gloss - Shiny, vibrant, most popular choice.

Matte - No shine, softer look, fingerprint resistant.

Satin - Subtle sheen between gloss and matte.

White vinyl - Best all-around choice. Crisp colors, very durable.

Clear - When you want the background to show through.

Holographic/Glitter/Mirror - When you want to stand out and get noticed.

Kraft - Natural, eco-friendly aesthetic.

Not sure? See all materials or order samples.

Shipping & Turnaround

Free shipping on orders over $75. Business accounts get free shipping on orders over $500.

Support

We guarantee your satisfaction. If there's a print defect, shipping damage, or any quality issue - we'll reprint or refund. Contact us within 14 days of delivery with photos of the issue.

Before proof approval: yes, no problem. After proof approval: contact us immediately. If we haven't started printing, we can usually accommodate changes. Once printing starts, changes aren't possible.

Email us at orders@12ozstickers.com. We respond within 24 hours on business days, usually much faster.
